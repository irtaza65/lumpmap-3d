## What the product actually is

LumpMap 3D is an anatomy-first educational navigator for visible or palpable superficial lumps. It is not a photo classifier, diagnostic chatbot, or location-to-diagnosis lookup table.

The experience has one connected journey:

1. Start with **body location** using a rotatable 3D body or accessible region list.
2. Change scale to a **procedural tissue cutaway** to learn what can differ beneath the skin.
3. Compare similar-looking lumps at a consistent anatomical scale.
4. Describe a concern in **English, Urdu, Roman Urdu, or guided questions**.
5. GPT-5.6 may normalize free text into a strict symptom record; it cannot diagnose or choose urgency.
6. Deterministic local rules produce emergency, same-day, prompt-appointment, or lower-risk guidance before educational matches.
7. Curated condition records provide the learning cards, medical sources, and review dates.
8. The Visit Note contains only facts supplied by the user.
