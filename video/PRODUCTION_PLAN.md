# LumpMap 3D — Build Week demo video

## Locked format

- **Runtime:** 2:48 (168 seconds)
- **Canvas:** 1920×1080, 16:9, 30 fps
- **Scenes:** 14
- **AI-generated clips:** 4 clips × 7 seconds = 28 seconds (16.7%)
- **Real product capture:** approximately 108 seconds (64.3%)
- **Remotion titles, diagrams, and end card:** approximately 32 seconds (19%)
- **Voice:** calm, warm, confident; 0.96–1.00× normal speed
- **Music:** optional, instrumental only, very low under narration, licensed for reuse

The real product stays dominant. AI video is used only for the opening hook and three explanatory transitions. All readable text, interface labels, arrows, and diagrams should be created in Remotion so they remain crisp and accurate.

## Visual system

- Background: warm off-white `#F3F1EC`
- Secondary surface: pale grey `#D8D5CF`
- Text: slate grey `#545B61` (never pure black)
- Fine lines: `#8D9296`
- Primary accent: muted terracotta `#CF7654`
- Secondary accent: muted sage/teal `#718E89`
- Urgency accent: restrained coral `#D86755`
- Corners: 20–28 px
- Shadows: soft, diffuse, low opacity
- Motion: After Effects-style spring easing, slow camera drift, clean mask reveals
- Avoid: black backgrounds, pure black text, neon, cyberpunk styling, gore, realistic wounds, glitch effects, fast zooms, fake UI text, and generated logos

## Recording checklist

- Use the current live public URL unless a local GPT-enabled build is intentionally shown.
- For the strongest GPT proof, run locally with a server-side `OPENAI_API_KEY`; never show the key or `.env.local` on screen.
- If the public Demo Mode is shown, keep the narration exactly as written: it honestly distinguishes the GPT-5.6 path from the local fallback.
- Set browser zoom to 90% or 100% and record at 1920×1080.
- Hide bookmarks, downloads, personal tabs, notifications, email addresses, API keys, and terminal paths containing private data.
- Turn on Do Not Disturb.
- Keep generated AI clip text-free; text will be overlaid in Remotion.
- Do not use copyrighted music or stock footage without an appropriate license.
- Export H.264 MP4, 1080p, 30 fps, target 12–20 Mbps.
- Final upload must be public on YouTube and remain under three minutes.
